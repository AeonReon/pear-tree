Our Menu

The Food Everyone's Talking About

Every dish is made fresh in our kitchen using quality ingredients. No shortcuts, no frozen nonsense — just proper homemade food the way it should be.

Legendary Pancakes

Fluffy pancakes stacked with smoked back bacon and drizzled in real maple syrup. Customers say they're "the best in Coleraine" — and we agree.

Eggs Benedict on Sourdough

Perfectly poached eggs with rich hollandaise on thick-cut sourdough toast. Our most Instagrammed dish for a reason.

Cinnamon French Toast

Thick slices of brioche dipped in cinnamon batter, golden-fried and served with fresh berries and cream. Pure indulgence.

Homemade Soups

A different soup every day, made fresh that morning and served with warm sourdough bread. Perfect comfort food.

Speciality Coffee

Expertly crafted Americanos, silky lattes, and oat milk options. Because great food deserves great coffee alongside it.

Fresh Bakes & Scones

Homemade fruit scones, cherry scones, and fresh pastries baked in-house every morning. Grab one with your coffee — you deserve it.

Our Story

More Than a Cafe — It's Where Coleraine Comes Together

The Pear Tree isn't just a place to eat — it's where friends catch up over pancakes, where families enjoy a lazy Saturday brunch, and where visitors discover the best food on the North Coast.

Everything on our menu is made from scratch, right here in our kitchen. We're passionate about quality ingredients, generous portions, and making sure every single person who walks through our door leaves happier than when they arrived.

And if you have dietary needs? We take gluten-free seriously. Our staff are trained on cross-contamination and can guide you through every option with confidence.

The People Behind the Pear Tree

Passionate About Real Food & Warm Hospitality

We started The Pear Tree because we believed Coleraine deserved a place where the food is always fresh, the coffee is always good, and the welcome is always warm.

Our team takes pride in every plate that leaves the kitchen. We know our regulars by name, and we treat first-timers like they've been coming for years.

We've recently expanded with The Orchard on Church Street — offering hot meals, traditional favourites, and local specialities. Because one place wasn't enough to share the love.
